More about this project:

I intend on building out this project more fully, but for now I wanted to experiment with creating an unconventional landing page for the training module.

Often, security training in companies can be very dry. Employees do not look forward to it and will often rush through it, not really absorbing the knowledge. I wanted to try a new approach to this training by using uncoventional images and layout.

As employees will complete the training at work, the pages can be more dyanmic and data intensive. That being said, I would like to create plain-text versions of the pages too to make them more accessible for all employees.
