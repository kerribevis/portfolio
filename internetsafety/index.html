More about this project:

I wanted to experiment with creating an unconventional theme for a training module.

Often, security training in companies can be very dry. Employees do not look forward to it and will often rush through it, not really absorbing the knowledge. I wanted to try a new approach to this training by using uncoventional images and layout.

In trying to keep the site as accessible as possible, I did not want to have too heavy a reliance on movement as well as images. I wanted to experiement more with color and with unconventional editing. Corporate training does not always need to be black and white.
